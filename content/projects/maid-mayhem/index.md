Maid Mayhem is a 2-4 player party brawler where big buff bald maids race to clean a house. They beat the gunk off the furniture to score points and beat the buff out of eachother to sabotage and steal points. Whoever has the most points at the end of the match wins, and is dubbed THE BEST MAID EVER!

## My Role

I was the **Technical Lead** on *Maid Mayhem*, leading a team of 5 programmers across an 11 person team working with art and design departments.
* Wrote and iterated on Technical Design Document, including APIs and UML diagrams.
* Architected all core systems.
* Built and iterated on all core gameplay systems: cleaning/combat, player controllers, match lifecycle.
* Scheduled and tracked weekly tasks using P4 Plan (Hansoft)
* Maintained the Git Repository

I also assisted in animation! I did all the cleaning transitional animations for the house furniture.
