## My Role

I was the **Producer** on *Hardly Workin'*, as well as a **Generalist Programmer**, **AI Programmer**, and **Animation Programmer**. My tasks involved:
* Leading a team of 7 through agile-style production, scheduling tasks using P4 Plan (Hansoft) and holding team meetings/check ins.
* Architect and implement the Boss and NPC AI behaviour
* Contributed directly to UI/UX, implementing menus and HUD
* Implemented all animation systems, VFX, and SFX
